One day I woke up hungry. I wanted a big ol' country breakfast, but I didn't feel like cooking when I first got up, and I sure didn't want to get showered and dressed so early on my one day off just to go out to a restaurant. I wished there were some chefs, or maybe wizards, that would make me breakfast. Alas, there were none. I looked though my local phonebook for a breadfast delivery place. Finding none, I asked a friend who works in the delivery food business why no one delivered breakfast.
